# Faith

Faith   
Faith a fading misconception of the mind    
Who are we? Nothing more than puppets of the mind   
Our physical bodies nothing but mimes   
Our emotions changing like uncontrollable tides   
We are nothing more than a glimpse in time   


























*Urangani T.M*
