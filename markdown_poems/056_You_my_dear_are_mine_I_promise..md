# You my dear are mine I promise.


 You my dear are mine I promise.  
I would give you the moon.  
But surely the sky would notice.  
From the aches of my heart  
I think you should know this.   
My love for you is real.  
Just wait for me to show it.  

























*Urangani T.M*
