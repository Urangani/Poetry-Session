# The world in colors

The world in colors    
Shaded by Hate a feeling so faint,    
an emotion no color can paint.   
A touch of jealousy,  what a terrible ink,   
spread the love use, the bucket of pink   

A dash of greed,  
to paint the money green,  
to those who don’t have it   
better plant the money trees  

To the one painting with the color of rage    
A misuse of the great color red,
