# Reality, A Colorful Illusion

**Author:** Urangani Terrence Mafunzwaini
**Preface:** Feelings may fade, but words endure. In these pages, joy and sorrow, beauty and despair, wisdom and folly are captured — not as permanent answers, but as echoes of moments that fade.

---

I wrote these poems out of curiosity, guided by a desire to explore the beauty of writing. Feelings may fade, and emotions slip quietly into forgetfulness, but literature holds them fast etched into memory by putting everything in paper. In these pages, I have carved the silly, the wonderful, and the romantic moments that once colored my life, preserving them in Reality, a Colorful Illusion. Through this act, I discovered not only the beauty of fleeting emotions, but also the measure of how deeply I could capture them.
